Mission

Trace Light is a technology initiative dedicated to locating missing persons through intelligent tracking devices and global cooperative networks.

We believe that every individual deserves to be found. Our platform connects families, investigators, and authorities with the tools they need to act swiftly and decisively.

Built with privacy and precision at its core, Trace Light represents a new standard in humanitarian technology.
